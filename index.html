About Me

Sanjeet Singh is a cinematographer/filmmaker currently based in India, previously based in Germany. A highly adept DOP with around 5 years of experience. Originally from Kanpur, he has worked across the length & breadth of India and various parts of Europe. His love for the visual medium propels his craft, and helps in creation of something meaningful for the viewer. His strongest suit still remains his conviction and belief in whatever is required for the project and understanding the basic philosophy of collective-collaborative effort in order to create. He recently completed his M.A. in Cinematography from MET FILM School, Berlin.
